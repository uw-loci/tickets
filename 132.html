Write a tutorial for making measurements in VisBio

Reported by: sorber Owned by: sorber
Priority: major Milestone:
Component: visbio Severity:
Keywords: Cc:
Blocked By: Blocking:

Description

The tutorial should describe the steps required to make and record measurements in VisBio. The tutorial should

  • Describe these steps at a high level of detail, pointing out individual menus and buttons that the user must click.
  • Use screen shots where applicable.
  • Be as brief as possible.

Important steps to cover:

  • Starting VisBio
  • Loading the desired images
  • Creating a display with overlays
  • Drawing overlays
  • Saving and loading overlays
  • Exporting overlays

Additional tasks to consider adding to the tutorial:

  • Repeating measurements at different rotations
