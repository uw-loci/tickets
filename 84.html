There are actually a total of three types of corrections to do:

  1. The deconvolution for each channel's lifetime histogram.
  2. A deskew of the spectral channel delays based on values in the calibration file.
  3. A scaling factor to apply to each spectral channel, to adjust for an "intensity skew" across channels.

The system response file will contain all of this information, once the engineers figure out the most reliable way to collect it.
