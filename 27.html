Description

Certain features of Slim Plotter require the ability to export to SDT format. Additionally, in the future, we will want to provide the ability to convert our new OME-HDF format to the legacy SDT format for use in SPCImage. As such, we will eventually need an SDT writer implementation within Bio-Formats.
