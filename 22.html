Data colorization is inconsistent

Reported by: curtis Owned by: curtis
Priority: minor Milestone:
Component: slim-plotter Severity:
Keywords: Cc:
Blocked By: Blocking:

Description

When toggling between "counts" and "lifetimes" colorization modes, the color autoscaling is often incorrect, resulting in (e.g.) a solidly colored data surface. Toggling between linear and log scales can also cause the problem to occur, or to disappear.

As a workaround, you can toggle back and forth between linear and log scales until the colors appear correctly (usually one or two iterations).
