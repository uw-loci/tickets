Align peaks needs to be more configurable

Reported by: curtis Owned by: nor
Priority: major Milestone:
Component: slim-plotter Severity: serious
Keywords: Cc:
Blocked By: Blocking:

Description (last modified by curtis) (diff)

The align peaks is sometimes wrong due to background noise. There need to be additional options for tweaking it: resetting the alignment, adjusting the alignment manually, etc.

If possible, all three of the initial checkboxes ("Align peaks", "Compute FWHMs" and "Cut 1.5ns from fit") should be migrated into the main GUI, so that people can change their minds about them later.

The lifetime and wavelength range information could be migrated into the main GUI, as well.
