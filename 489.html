Andor TIFF timestamps are missing or incorrect

Reported by: melissa Owned by: melissa
Priority: major Milestone: bio-formats-4.2
Component: bio-formats Severity: serious
Keywords: Cc: p.nanda@…
Blocked By: Blocking:

Description 

However even the OME timestamp metadata does not seem to meaningfully correspond to the timing values as seen in the iQ software. 
In the uploaded Andor multi-TIFF image “MOVIE1A_BFLAT.tif” there are 250 frames in total, but in XML only 50 of the Planes have PlaneTiming DeltaT.
The dataset tested has 4 dimensions:
- Time (5 repeats)
- XY (5 fields)
- Z (10 planes)
- Wavelength (1 wavelength)
Also no Name=Timestamp fields are in OriginalMetadata unlike “prettyimage.tif” which is just CT and imports timestamps as expected.

See files in data/andor/pariksheet.
